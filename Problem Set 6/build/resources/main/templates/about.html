About Us

Here is information about us

Dark Side of the Rainbow was established in 1978 by the late Dr. Bowrain Kard. A virtuoso at heart and someone with a passion for Darkwave music, Dr. Kard set up a business of selling his own music albums to the masses. After Dr. Kard's demise, his son, Edis Kard has taken over the family business.

Aside from having an array of music albums on our shelves for sale, our music store also leases out albums at a discounted price on special occasions. Make sure to drop by our website or store to check if we are offering any exclusive deals.
